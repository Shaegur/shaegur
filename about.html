ABOUT ME

Welcome To Shaegur's Journey So Far

My journey started when I first got my hands on a computer and began exploring how games worked behind the scenes. I was fascinated by the way code could bring characters, mechanics, and entire worlds to life. As I grew older, my curiosity turned into a passion for programming, leading me to learn different languages and experiment with game development tools. Every project I worked on reinforced my love for problem-solving and creativity, pushing me to improve and refine my skills. Now, my goal is to create immersive and engaging experiences that not only bring joy to players but also challenge me to keep learning and innovating.

Currently I am a student at St Dominics Sixth Form College in Harrow On The Hill, where I am studying 4 A-Level, which iclude: Mathematics, Further Mathematics, Computer Science and Physics. I am passionate about these 4 subject which I have chosen as they expand my knowleadge on subjects which have always interested me, and help with my problem-solving skills.

Before that, I went to De Salis Studio College in Hayes, where I studies 13 GCSEs and 2 Qualifications.
I acquired grade 9s in the following subjects: Mathematics, Computer Science, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Business and Economics.
I recieved grade 8s in the following subjects: Further Mathematics, Statistics, Geography, Citizenship and English Literature. Finally, I got a grade 7 in English Language.
The Qualifications which I achieved was a Merit in AAT Level 2 Certificate in Bookkeeping, and a Level 2 Distinction in Technical Award in Finance(TAF).
